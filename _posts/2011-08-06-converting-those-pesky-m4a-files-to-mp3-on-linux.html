--- layout: post title: Converting those pesky .m4a files to .mp3 on Linux categories: - Linux - Opensource tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true comments: true meta: _edit_last: '1' --- Here is a nice one-liner to convert a .m4a audio file to a .mp3 file: {% highlight sh %}$ faad -o - input_file.m4a | lame - output_file.mp3{% endhighlight %} Note: Make sure you have lame and faad installed. In debian they are just 'faad' and 'lame' packages.
